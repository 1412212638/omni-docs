This Acceptable Use Policy supplements the [Terms of Service](/en/legal/terms) and applies to all activity conducted through the OmniRouters website, console, APIs, documentation, and related tools.

## 1. Basic Principle

You may use OmniRouters only for lawful, legitimate, authorized, and policy-compliant purposes. You may not use the platform to conduct, facilitate, scale, or evade any unlawful, infringing, fraudulent, or abusive activity.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

You may not use OmniRouters to generate, process, store, distribute, or support:

- unlawful, infringing, deceptive, or fraudulent content
- defamatory, harassing, threatening, hateful, violent, or clearly harmful content

### 2.2 Security and Platform Abuse

You may not:

- bypass authentication, rate limits, permissions, or other security controls
- scan, probe, attack, overload, scrape, mirror, or interfere with the platform or related infrastructure
- distribute malware, ransomware, botnet controls, credential theft tools, or other malicious software

### 2.3 Fraud and Improper Commercial Conduct

You may not use OmniRouters for:

- phishing, fake support scams, payment fraud, spam, traffic manipulation, or deceptive marketing
- concealing or falsifying the true source of requests, payment responsibility, or business identity
- activity that violates payment network rules, sanctions obligations, export restrictions, or other applicable regulations

### 2.4 High-Risk or Sensitive Uses

Without appropriate safeguards, independent review, and legal authority where required, you may not rely on OmniRouters outputs as the sole basis for:

- employment, dismissal, performance evaluation, or education admissions
- lending, credit, insurance, housing, or public benefit eligibility decisions
- medical diagnosis, treatment decisions, legal advice, or law enforcement judgments
- identity verification, live surveillance, sensitive profiling, or other high-risk automated decisions

### 2.5 Intellectual Property and Data Compliance

You may not:

- upload or process data, code, documents, models, or assets that you are not authorized to use
- process third-party personal data without an adequate legal basis, notice, or authorization
- instruct OmniRouters to circumvent product restrictions, third-party rules, or legal obligations
